Goal 3

Good Health and Well-being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages

Key Targets

  • Reduce maternal mortality ratio
  • End preventable deaths of newborns and children
  • Combat communicable diseases
  • Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases
  • Strengthen prevention of substance abuse

Local Challenges

  • Healthcare accessibility in rural areas
  • Mental health service gaps
  • Aging population care needs
  • Health inequality in disadvantaged communities

Implementation Plan

1

Assessment

Evaluate current health infrastructure and identify gaps in service delivery

2

Planning

Develop targeted interventions and resource allocation strategies

3

Implementation

Execute health programs and monitor progress

4

Evaluation

Measure outcomes and adjust strategies based on results

Policy Recommendations

Healthcare Access

Implement mobile health units for rural areas and expand telemedicine services

Mental Health

Integrate mental health services into primary care and increase funding for community programs

Prevention

Strengthen early detection programs and health education initiatives
